About FLA

FLA stands for Female Leadership Academy. I recently joined in September 2017. We learn and practice the skills of being a leader. On Wednesdays we do yoga, circuit traning and learn about foods. These help us learn more about our body and feel more comfortable in our own skin. In FLA we takes steps that lead us to becoming better people. We do little projects to help us grow. We have developed some skills such as:

  • Public speaking
  • Team building
  • Problem solving
  • Friendship
  • Having confidence

How it has helped me

Female Leadership Academy has helped me in so many ways. It's helped me feel more comfortable in my own body. I have also learbed a lot on what it means to be a leader. I have been able to see things in a different ways and think more positive. You have to take on a huge role because others look up to you. It has also helped me handle different situations more better. I've really improved in being a better person and using the skills that leaders use.
